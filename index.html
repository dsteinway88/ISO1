    Launch of the OnGuard Security Awareness Initiative - April 2015

    Last year, more than 70 lawyers and law firms were duped out of over $29 million dollars by way of a phishing scam requesting the firm’s help in collecting an outstanding judgment.

    Industry experts estimate that more than 80 major law firms were hacked last year.

    The FBI has noted that law firms are increasingly the target of hackers and cybersecurity threats because they are rich in sensitive and protected information, like financial data, medical records, and even trade secrets.

    The headlines are impossible to ignore. Cybersecurity even made its way into this year’s State of the Union address. And even though a certain amount of data security relies on technology and equipment, the behavior and work practices of our employees play an equally important role in ensuring our Firm’s data remains secure. Whether it’s using smart passwords, making sure we’re using secure tools to transfer files, or locking our mobile devices, we all have a role to play when it comes to data security.

    With that in mind, Dechert pleased to introduce OnGuard, our new security awareness program. This program will not only give us insight into many of the pressing security issues in our industry today, but also provide us guidance around best practices that help us work in ways intended to keep our information secure.

    The OnGuard program will kick off with a the introduction of two learning key modules which all employees will be asked to view. The program also includes a number of resources for your ongoing reference, as well as online content focused on keeping our firm, and our clients’ data, safe. You’ll learn more details about the program’s launch in an upcoming emails, and through frequent updates to this site.

    Unfortunately, it seems security threats are here to stay and are evolving at an increasing rate. We believe the best defense is a good offense, and the OnGuard initiative is intended to prepare our employees with the knowledge they need to work safely in service to our clients.
