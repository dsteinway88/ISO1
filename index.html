Alerts and Updates

Recent updates and notices.

Alert: Jan 8 2015 - We have experienced an email spoofing incident against the
Dechert internet domain. Recipients received emails containing a fake ‘Notice to Appear
at court’ message. These emails are SPAM messages that did not come from Dechert or anyone
affiliated with Dechert. If you received one or more of these emails, please do not open
them – we ask instead that you delete them immediately.
More information can be found here: http://bit.ly/K9bxc6.

Please contact the Help Desk or email ITSecurity if you need assistance.
